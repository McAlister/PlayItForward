Thank You All!

Quick shout out to our corporate sponsors. We love these guys.

Red Deck Wins:

Red Deck Wins provides high quality & limited edition apparel for cardboard spellslingers. They will begin a monthly donation of a portion of their proceeds to Play It Forward in August as well as 100% of the profits from Play It Forward branded apparel.

Gamermats:

If you can't find the perfect playmat, GamerMats can make you a mat with your artwork and have it in your hands in about a week. And they are where our gorgeous prize playmats come from.

Merkerinn:

Merkerinn is an amazingly talented freelance graphic artist from Poland who has donated the bulk of our playmat art for 2017. Check out her Patreon to see other works of art and also streams of her creation process.
