GP Pittsburgh:

It seems like San Jose was moments ago but we are already at GP Pittsburgh! Sign up to let us know you are competing. Please fill out your name as it appears on the standings boards. If you want you can email us as well to ask for a spot in our beta test of push notifications of pairings.

The top female/nonbinary player will receive a gorgeous playmat featuring artwork by Malwina Kwiatkowska (merkerinn on facebook). She's a gifted digital artist from Poland who specializes in concept art, 3D modeling, and fan art. She also streams her work on Twitch, which is pretty dang cool! As always the mats themselves are provided by our heroes at GamerMats. Check them out if you want a playmat that is all your own.

But that is not all. Erin Campbell of The Girlfriend Bracket and Magic Mics podcasts is donating one box of Shadows Over Innistrad and none other than Platinum Pro Sam Pardee is donating a modo mentorship. Sam will find a time to skype with the winner to discuss drafting tactics in this set as well as provide 70 tickets for up to 5 drafts.

None of the folks at Play It Forward can make it to Pittsburgh, but generally awesome judge Bryan Spellman will be holding the playmat and if anyone else wants to donate something on site you can look for him to drop it off.

So ladies, are you ready? Go forth and conquer.
