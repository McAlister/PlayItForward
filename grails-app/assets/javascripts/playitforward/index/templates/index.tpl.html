Welcome to Play It Forward. Our goal? Get more women on the Pro Tour.

We seek to promote and cultivate women playing Magic: the Gathering at a competitive level by sponsoring additional prizes that women can compete for in high level play and by keeping track of statistics of women's performances at the near-pro level.
