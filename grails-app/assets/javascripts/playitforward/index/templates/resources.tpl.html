Ladies On the Grind:

LoTG is a facebook group for women who aspire to Magic greatness. Share tips, triumphs and travel costs as you pursue the elusive top 8 finish.

Lady Planeswalker Society:

Breaking down the barriers of entry into Magic is the number one goal of the group, but we also aim to provide a stepping-stone into tournament play. With over 100 chapters in the world, this group has been very successful and continues to grow. The group teaches Magic at many conventions with a large, dedicated staff of experienced teachers.

Planeswalkers For Diversity:

Our main focus has been on facilitating the creation of real life playgroups that emphasize a welcoming atmosphere for players of all kinds.

Collected Company:

The mission of the Collected Company project is to empower women to take on new challenges and realize their full potential within the Judge Program.

Liga das Garotas Mágicas:

The League of Magical Girls is the Brazilian version of Ladies on the Grind. If you are a woman who loves magic and speaks portuguese check them out.

Limited Resources:

A weekly podcast dedicated to improving your play at Magic: The Gathering with an emphasis on Limited play.

Constructed Resources:

A much less frequent podcast Where you come to improve at Magic: the Gathering, with a focus on Constructed play.

Allied Strategies:

Another podcast about constructed Magic.

MTG Pro Tutor:

MTG Pro Tutor is dedicated to increasing the skill of Magic players everywhere by delving into the minds of our community’s greatest players.

Top Level:

More constructed deck talk with Hall of Fame player Patrick Chapin and strategy writer Michael Flores

WoTC Event Master Schedule:

The Gathering events are held around the world every year. Find a location near you and see how you stack up against the best players in the world!

PPTQ Schedule:

Preliminary Pro Tour Qualifiers (PPTQs) are the first step for new players on their path to the Magic: The Gathering Pro Tour. By winning a Preliminary PTQ, you will qualify for that season’s Regional Pro Tour Qualifier (RPTQ).

RPTQ Schedule:

Regional Pro Tour Qualifiers (RPTQs) are your chance to play on the Magic: The Gathering Pro Tour! Top finishers at a Regional PTQ will receive an invitation – and airfare – to the Pro Tour that it qualifies for!

Grand Prixs:

Grand Prixs are the biggest non-invitational tournaments run by WOTC. With 15 rounds over two days they offer Pro Points and invitations to the Pro Tour as well as cash prizes to roughly the top 64.

Star City Opens:

Complete schedule for Star City Open tournaments. They are a lot like Grand Prix's only they offer invitations to the Star City Invitational instead of the WoTC Pro Tour.
